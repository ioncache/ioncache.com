Page Info

The decision to use just regular HTML, vanilla JS (mostly), and vanilla CSS to create this page was made as I didn't want to pick a specific framework.

I think sometimes developers get too attached to their framework of choice, and can get tunnel vision when trying to decide what to use for a new project.

Generally I'm framework agnostic as they all have their pros and cons.

A Little Bit About Me

Over the years I've gravitated toward companies where the work has a tangible upside for real people -- reducing food waste, helping conserve energy, improving access to healthcare. That's where I want to keep going.

On the technical side I work best as a generalist across the full stack, and mentorship is a big part of how I operate. Watching someone work through a hard problem and come out the other side is one of my favorite parts of the job.

I'm spending a lot of time thinking about AI too -- how to integrate it thoughtfully and where it actually adds value, rather than just adds complexity. The hype may be a bit overblown, but genuinely excited about what it can do when it's used well.
